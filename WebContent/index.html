About Us


--- Our Story ---

ManyBooks was established in 2004 with the vision to provide an extensive library of books in digital format for free on the Internet. Many of the early eBooks are from the Project Gutenberg archives, which means you will be able to find a lot of classics on the site.

Over a decade later, we are still going strong in our mission to provide a valuable service to our readers. ManyBooks has grown into a platform where self-publishing authors have the opportunity to introduce their work to our community and new books are uploaded to our site daily - provided they comply to our quality standards.We strive for customer satisfaction by offering a user-friendly search engine, efficient payment options and seamless delivery systems. Apart from all this, we also provide great deals and discounts on our products.

We offer a huge collection of books from diverse categories of Fiction, Non-fiction, Biographies, History, Religion, Self-Help, etc. We also offer a collection of Investments and Management, Computers, Engineering, Medical, College and School text reference books.

--- Founder ---

ManyBooks was founded in 2017 by Virajas Kulkarni, a native of Katraj. he done his graduation in BA Literature from Bharati Vadidyapeeth in 2016. he has fond of books and he is writer. With $300 borrowed from his parents, he opened a small shop selling used and bargain general interest books at Bharati Vidyapeeth, Katraj.

In today's Technological age, Everyone is using internet for every daily tasks, so we launched our website for Books Shoping it makes customer easy to buy books. Ordinary stores have a limited area for displaying books, but internet stores have massive databases. When you shop for your favorite book online, you save money, energy, and time.
